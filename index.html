01 · ABOUT

Print-hardened.
Remote-ready.

I'm Jefferson — a print production veteran from San Jose del Monte, Bulacan. I ran J3FF Printing Services across 3 branches for over a decade. Every day I worked with files that couldn't be redone once they hit the press. You learn fast what "good enough" actually costs.

Now I'm taking that same precision online. Most VAs handle email and scheduling. I do that too — but I can also catch a low-res bleed error, rebuild a broken PDF form, design a booklet from scratch, and automate the whole output with a Puppeteer pipeline.

I work at the intersection of print craft and digital tools. I speak both the design language and the technical one. One specialist instead of three.
